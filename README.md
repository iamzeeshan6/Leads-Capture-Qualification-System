his automated workflow captures real estate leads through a Typeform submission and processes them through an AI-based qualification system using predefined filters. Each lead is evaluated based on three criteria: budget (≥ 20,000), timeline (within 3 months), and buyer readiness (pre-approved mortgage or cash buyer). 

Qualified leads are automatically: 

Stored in the Qualified Leads Sheet 

Added to the tracking system 

Sent a custom-styled email notification 

Unqualified or general leads are stored in a separate Generic Leads Sheet for record-keeping and future follow-up.
